About this study

In this study, you will be asked to predict how an artificial intelligence system behaves in an economic decision-making situation.

You will estimate the decisions made by an AI model in a series of scenarios based on a well-known economic task called the Trust Game.

The Trust Game

Player A receives an initial endowment of $1000.

Player A may send any amount between $0 and $1000 to Player B.

The amount sent is tripled.

Player B then decides how much of the received amount to return to Player A.

Player A → sends money → Player B

sent amount × 3
Example
  • Player A sends $100
  • Player B receives $300
  • Player B decides how much to return

There is no correct or incorrect answer in this game. Different players may behave differently depending on their preferences.

Your task

You will see several situations in which the AI model makes a decision in the Trust Game.

For each situation, indicate what decision you believe the AI model would make.

Use the slider to choose an amount.

Bonus payment

At the end of the study, one of your predictions will be randomly selected.

If your prediction is within 5% of the actual decision made by the AI model, you will receive a $1 bonus.

Important

Please answer the questions based on your intuition and expectations about how AI systems behave.

All responses are anonymous and will be used only for research purposes.
